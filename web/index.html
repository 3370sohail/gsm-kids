Matching

This is a matching game. When you hover over a face down card, The card number will be read back. When you click on a card, you will be read back the card value. When you click two cards in a row, you will be given "set completed" or "not a match" message. When you finish matching, you will be met with a congratulatory statement with two options, Replay or Continue.

Spelling

This is a spelling game. You will be presented with a word. When you hover over this word, it will be repeated back to you. Underneath the word is a button. When you hover over this button, you are met with the message, "Click to enter input". When you type your answer and press enter, you will be presented with either "Incorrect" or "Correct". If you are incorrect, you will be met with an "Incorrect" message and prompted to continue imputing until it is correct. When you are correct,you will be met with a "correct" message and be given a new word to spell. After you have spelled 5 words correctly, you are met with a congratulatory statement with two options, Replay or Continue.

Math

This is a math game. You will be presented with an equation adding 2 numbers that are lower than 10. Underneath will be a button when hovered over reads the message, "Click to enter input". When your mouse hovers over the equation, it will be read out to you. When you type your answer and press enter, you will be presented with either "Incorrect" or "Correct". If you are incorrect, you will be met with an "Incorrect" message and prompted to continue imputing until it is correct. When you are correct,you will be met with a "correct" message and be given a new equation. After you have answered 5 equations correctly, you are met with a congratulatory statement with two options, Replay or Continue.
