This is a math game. You will be presented with an equation adding 2 numbers 
that are lower than 10. Underneath will be a button when hovered over reads 
the message, "Click to enter input". When your mouse hovers over the equation, 
it will be read out to you. When you type your answer and press enter, you will 
be presented with either "Incorrect" or "Correct". If you are incorrect, you will 
be met with an "Incorrect" message and prompted to continue imputing until it is 
correct.  When you are correct,you will be met with a "correct" message and be 
given a new equation. After you have answered 5 equations correctly, you
are met with a congratulatory statement with two options, Replay or Continue.
