This is a spelling game. You will be presented with a word. When you hover over 
this word, it will be repeated back to you. Underneath the word is a button. When 
you hover over this button, you are met with the message, "Click to enter input". 
When you type your answer and press enter, you will 
be presented with either "Incorrect" or "Correct". If you are incorrect, you will 
be met with an "Incorrect" message and prompted to continue imputing until it is 
correct.  When you are correct,you will be met with a "correct" message and be 
given a new word to spell. After you have spelled 5 words correctly, you 
are met with a congratulatory statement with two options, Replay or Continue.
